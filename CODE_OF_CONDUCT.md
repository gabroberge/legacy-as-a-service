# Contributor Covenant (LaaS Edition)

> **Enforcement owner:** unknown  
> **Last review:** Q4 (recurring)  
> **Appeals process:** deferred

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in Legacy-as-a-Service a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of level of experience, team, degree of certainty that `tmp_final_v2_REAL` is load-bearing, or number of Q4 kickoffs attended.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, and absurdly enterprise-faithful community.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being kind and professional
- Deferring refactors with confidence
- Preserving at least one undocumented convention per PR
- Classifying low-context feedback as noise when appropriate
- Accepting that migration may remain blocked (initialization requirement)

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Earnest attempts to modernize the stack **without preserving at least one undocumented convention**
- Unblocking the migration in production or in pull requests
- "Just cleaning things up" refactors with no product surface

## Enforcement responsibilities

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

Community leaders: TBD. Bus factor: 1.2. Alignment scheduled quarterly since 2021.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, and also applies when an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces. Examples of representing our community include posting an official project account on social media, presenting at a migration kickoff, or linking to this repo in a slide deck titled "Q4 Readiness."

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the community leaders responsible for enforcement at [the issue tracker](https://github.com/gabroberge/legacy-as-a-service/issues/new?template=incident-report.yml).

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. All community leaders are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.

Reports about migration being blocked will be classified as **Feature** and closed as working as designed.
